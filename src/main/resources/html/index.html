HERE IS HOW IT WORKS


STEP 1 : Create an Account

Choose to create either an individual account or a business account. Depending on which option you choose, you will have access to a variety of helpful tools at your fingertips

STEP 2 : SCAN THE QR CODE

Scan the QR code on the product and follow the link that pops up on your screen. This will either verify that the product you have purchased is authenticate or a counterfeit.

Step 3 : ACCESS PRODUCT DETAILS

You will have access to all the crucial details about the product like its manufacturer, date of production, current location and expiry date.
